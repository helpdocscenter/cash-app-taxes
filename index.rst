Introduction to Cash App Taxes
-------------------------------

**Cash App Taxes** (formerly Credit Karma Tax) is a free tax filing service built directly into Cash App. It allows U.S. taxpayers to file returns without the hidden charges or upgrade fees common on other platforms.

Key benefits include:

- **100% free filing** for federal and state returns  
- **Simple and fast setup** through your Cash App  
- **Supports major IRS forms** including W-2, 1099-NEC, 1099-INT, 1099-DIV, Schedule C, and more  
- **Accurate calculations** backed by built-in error checking  
- **Direct deposit refunds** straight into Cash App balance  
- **Secure data encryption** and identity protection  

If you're looking for an easier way to file your taxes in 2025, Cash App Taxes is a trusted and beginner-friendly solution.

--------------------------------
Who Can Use Cash App Taxes?
--------------------------------

Cash App Taxes is ideal for taxpayers with:

- Simple and moderate tax situations  
- Employment income (W-2)  
- Freelance or gig income (1099-NEC, 1099-K)  
- Investment income (stocks, crypto)  
- Business expenses (Schedule C)  
- Standard or itemized deductions  

**Important:**  
Some complex tax situations (like multi-state filings or rental property depreciation) may not be supported. See the “Forms” section for full details.

----------------------------------------------
What You Need Before Filing (Quick Checklist)
----------------------------------------------

Before starting, make sure you have:

- Your **Cash App account** fully verified  
- Social Security Number or ITIN  
- Your W-2 from your employer  
- Any 1099 forms for freelance/contract work  
- Investment statements (if applicable)  
- Previous year's tax return (optional)  
- Bank info for refunds (optional, Cash App Direct Deposit also available)  

Once your documents are ready, you can begin filing in minutes.

-----------------------------------------------
How to Get Started With Cash App Tax Filing
-----------------------------------------------

1. Open Cash App on your device  
2. Tap the profile icon  
3. Select Taxes  
4. Tap Start a new return  
5. Answer initial verification and identity questions  
6. Begin entering your tax documents  
7. Review your tax return summary  
8. Submit your federal and state return for free  

The process is streamlined to reduce confusion and ensure accuracy.
